Privacy Policy

At Raju Marvari, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when using our platform at rajumarvari.com.

Information We Collect

We collect information to enhance your experience, including:

  • Personal Details: Name, email, phone number, and address.
  • Account Data: Username, password, and profile preferences.
  • Order Information: Food preferences, transaction details, and order history.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device details.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, actions taken, and time spent on the platform.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your food orders.
  • To offer customer service and respond to your inquiries.
  • To improve our platform’s performance and user experience.
  • To send order updates and transactional notifications (we do not send promotional messages without your consent).
  • To detect and prevent fraud and enhance security.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data to third parties. We may share your information only under these conditions:

  • Delivery Partners: Only limited information like your phone number may be shared to coordinate delivery.
  • Service Providers: For payment gateway and customer service support.
  • Legal Obligations: When required by law or government agencies.

Location & Photo Usage

Our logistics partners are not permitted to store, download, or misuse these images in any form.

Security Measures

We use industry-standard security protocols to safeguard your data. However, no digital platform is entirely immune to threats. We encourage users to protect their account credentials.

Your Rights

  • Access and update your personal data.
  • Request data deletion.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
