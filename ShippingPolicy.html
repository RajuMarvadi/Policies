Shipping Policy

1. Delivery Areas

We deliver within a 10km radius of Madhapur, including Gachibowli and Nanakramguda. Additional charges may apply for areas outside this zone. Customers will be informed of any delays.

2. Delivery Schedule

Deliveries are made daily. Please place your order at least 3 hours in advance. Corporate orders will be delivered at the time specified during ordering.

3. Delivery Fees

Currently, delivery is free. This is subject to change and will be updated in our policy if revised.

4. Order Tracking

Once dispatched, you'll receive a notification with an estimated delivery time. You can track your order via the "My Orders" section in the app.

5. Special Instructions

Please add any delivery notes (e.g., landmarks or gate codes) while placing your order to help us serve you better.

6. Food Safety

All meals are prepared in hygienic conditions and securely packed to maintain freshness and safety during delivery.

7. Logistics

We work with trusted third-party logistics partners. They are only authorized to contact customers regarding delivery.
